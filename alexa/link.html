Link a Station

Connect a personal weather station to StationCast on Alexa

Enter your weather station ID to link it to your Alexa account. You can find your station ID in the StationCast app under Settings → Manage Stations, or on your Weather Underground dashboard.

If set, you can say "Alexa, ask StationCast for the weather at Home."

Try again

This page is part of the Alexa account-linking flow. To link a station, open the Alexa app, find the StationCast skill, and tap Link Account.
