# Support

<p class="page-meta">Get help with StationCast</p>

Need a hand, hit a bug, or have a request? Send it through our support form and
we'll get back to you.

**[Open the StationCast support form →](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfpXVVROkucuNBB0lk7jQplcAkO4P1hIexUeb6E7zYf-y1vpw/viewform)**

Prefer email? Reach us at
[evandhoffman@gmail.com](mailto:evandhoffman@gmail.com).

and your **iOS version**.
